History

White Water Rafting Co. was founded in the summer of 2018 when Mr. Walter White went out to Wisconsin for the summer holidays. What started as a hobby has now developed into a community with a common goal: To deliver unforgettable rafting experiences that combine safety, excitement, and nature.
